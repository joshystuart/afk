# Session Prompt: Repo Onboarding

Give me a fast but accurate walkthrough of this codebase.

Focus on:

- the main app flow
- how sessions are created and managed
- how scheduled jobs are executed
- the most important frontend pages and backend services
- 3 good places for a new contributor to start

End with a short mental model of how everything fits together.

## Good Follow-Up

Now narrow that down: what are the top 3 areas most likely to break during future changes, and why?
